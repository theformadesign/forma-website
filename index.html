    FAQs

    Questions, answered.

    Can I edit the website after delivery? +

    Yes. We deliver clean, well-commented HTML files. You can edit text, swap images, or add sections yourself. For bigger changes, opt for our monthly maintenance add-on.

    What if I want more than 5 pages? +

    Easy. Each additional page is ₹2,500. We'll quote your total based on the final page count before we start.

    Do I need to buy hosting and domain myself? +

    Yes — you'll own them. Domains usually cost ₹600–₹1,200/year. Hosting from ₹100/month. We're happy to guide you through choosing and setting them up.

    Will my website work on mobile? +

    Absolutely. Every package includes full mobile responsiveness — phone, tablet, desktop, big monitors. We design and test on every screen size.

    Can I see designs before approving everything? +

    Yes — we share PDF + PNG previews of each page before coding. Once you approve the visuals, we move to development. No surprises.

    What if I need a logo too? +

    Check out our Brand Identity packages. Bundle a website with logo design and we'll give you a sweet combo discount.

    Do you provide refunds? +

    The 50% advance is non-refundable as it covers wireframes and initial design work. With unlimited revisions, we ensure you're satisfied with the final outcome.
